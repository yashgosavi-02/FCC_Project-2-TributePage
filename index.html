Mr.Ratan Tata

Most Respected Man in India.

Here's a time line of Mr.Ratan Tata:

  • 1937 - Born on December 28, 1937, in Mumbai, India, into the Tata family, a renowned business family.
  • 1962 - Completed his B.S. degree in Architecture from Cornell University, USA.
  • 1962-1971 - Started his career working in various Tata Group companies, including Tata Steel, Tata Motors, and Tata Power.
  • 1971 - Appointed as the Director-in-Charge of the National Radio & Electronics Company Limited (NELCO), a Tata subsidiary.
  • 1981 - Becomes the Chairman of Tata Industries, the holding company of Tata Group.
  • 1991 - Appointed as the Chairman of Tata Sons, the parent company of the Tata Group, after the retirement of J.R.D. Tata.
  • 1998 - Launched the Tata Indica, India's first indigenously developed passenger car.
  • 2000 -Spearheaded Tata Group's global expansion by acquiring Tetley, a renowned British tea company.
  • 2000 -Received Padma Bhushan, from Indian Government.
  • 2007 - Acquired Jaguar Land Rover (JLR) from Ford Motor Company, marking a significant milestone in Tata Group's automotive business.
  • 2008 - The Tata Nano, the world's cheapest car, was launched by Tata Motors. It aims to provide affordable transportation to the masses.
  • 2008 - Received Padma Vibhushan, from Government of India.
  • 2012 - Steped down as the Chairman of Tata Sons, and Cyrus Mistry was appointed as his successor.
  • 2021 - Announced his retirement from Tata Trusts, one of India's largest philanthropic organizations, after serving as its Chairman for over two decades
  • 2023 - Received Order of Australia.

    • "A visionary leader, an embodiment of innovation, and a driving force behind India's progress."
